How SustainAbite Works

Seamlessly connecting surplus food with those who need it most.

Home How It Works Features Membership Contact Suggestions

1. Register & Verify

Food providers (restaurants, caterers, canteens) and receivers (NGOs, kitchens, volunteers) easily sign up on our app. All partners are verified for safety and trust.

2. List Surplus Food

Providers list extra food anytime, entering details like type, quantity, preparation time, and hygiene certification. Photos can be added for clarity.

3. Real-Time Notifications

Nearby NGOs and helpers receive instant alerts about available food, with logistics and pickup instructions included.

4. Schedule & Track Pickup

Receivers request pickup; SustainAbite coordinates timing and offers live tracking to ensure timely delivery and safety.

5. Make an Impact

Food reaches those in need, while contributors receive delivery updates, fostering trust and gratitude.
