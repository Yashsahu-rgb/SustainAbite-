How Annsetu Works

Seamlessly connecting surplus food with those who need it most.

Home How It Works Features Membership Contact Suggestions

1. Register & Verify

Food providers (restaurants, caterers, canteens) and receivers (NGOs, kitchens, volunteers) easily sign up on our app. All partners are verified for safety and trust.

2. List Surplus Food

Providers list extra food at any time, entering details like type, quantity, and preparation time, plus hygiene certification. The process is quick and supports photos.

3. Real-Time Notifications

Nearby NGOs and helpers receive instant alerts about available food in their vicinity, along with logistics and pickup instructions.
